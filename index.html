Interesting Facts About The Camel

  • Camels can survive for a long time without water
  • Camels have specially designed feet that help them move across sand
  • Camel's humps store food and water

Interesting Facts About The Chameleon

  • Many Chameleons can change color
  • Chameleons can camouflage to hide from predators and kill prey
  • Chameleons have slippery skin

Interesting Facts About The Cicada

  • There are around 3000 cicada species
  • Cicadas are herbivores
  • Cicadas are related to butterflies
